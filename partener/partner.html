Partnerships and Collaboration


In order to provide the highest quality car rental services and the continuous development of the car rental market in Moldova, we invite for collaboration natural and legal persons who have a car fleet that can be offered for rent. Our company, ICAB Group S.R.L., offers car rental services, aiming to provide niche services with a first-class experience, which are related to the activity we carry out.

Over the years, the CarsRent.md company has positioned itself on the local car market as one of the most important and reliable operators in the Car Rentals sector in the Republic of Moldova, developing new relationships and opportunities and offering high-quality services at competitive prices .


Currently, our company is proud of a rich experience in the field, a well-trained and qualified staff, a physical presence with an office and car park, and a presence in the online environment, being a leader in this segment, as well as with a varied car park, which includes cars from family class models, such as minivans like Volkswagen Atlas, Toyota Highlander, and Skoda Kodiaq, to luxury and business class brands, such as Mercedes Benz, BMW, and Audi. Thus, depending on the budget, needs, and personal preferences, each customer can easily choose the perfect car.


The main values of the RentCarsMD company are respect, passion and excellence in what we do.


At the same time, our company offers additional services to customers, such as:

  • free delivery of the reserved car within the Chisinau city limits, with the signing of the rental documents directly at the time of taking over the car, to the address mentioned by the customer;

  • extra-schedule delivery, if the customer's flight is delayed or takes place late;

  • transfer from Chisinau International Airport, if the client requests to be met at the airport to be driven to the company's office and sign the requested car rental contract;

  • transfer to Chisinau International Airport, if at the time of returning the rented car, the customer requests to be provided with a transfer to the airport;

  • the child seat, for a car rental as comfortable and carefree as possible.


Reasons to collaborate with us

  • All the care of the means of transport is on us, you don't have to think about what and when it needs to be repaired, changed or adjusted.

  • Maximum transparency when the car is rented, ensuring the delivery and reception of the car in the best possible conditions, and if irregularities are detected, these irregularities are removed

  • One of the highest ROI percentages

  • Contracts signed with full fulfillment of commitments

  • CASCO car insurance to avoid risks and expenses

  • Regular car testing to prevent car breakdowns


Taking into account the values promoted by our company, the results achieved, the high performance and quality of the services provided to our customers, we believe that a future collaboration with our partners will develop and maintain long-term and advantageous relationships, both for you and for us.
